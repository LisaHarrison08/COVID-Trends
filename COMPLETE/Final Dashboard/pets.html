Pet Adoption

As humans learn how to adapt to the new restrictions and changing lifestyles brought about by COVID-19 , pets in the United States continue to be born, surrendered to shelters, and adopted into loving homes. These graphs represent data from 1200 animal welfare organizations around the country and compare weekly data from 2019 and 2020.
